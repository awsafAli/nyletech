Our Story

WAMLOR is a start-up which focuses mainly on bringing in products and solutions keeping in mind the ecological imbalance which has been gradually leading to the destruction of mother nature. Based out of Mangalore, we are a team of three young energetic engineers. We decided to tackle the problem associated with the use of plastic bottles. Putting in months of hard work, we designed ‘TURTLE’ by WAMLOR – a high quality borosilicate water bottle with a breath taking silicone sleeve design. This bottle is class apart. It will not just change the way people drink or carry water but will revolutionize the world of water bottles. When you own one, you care about nature and also your health. This is how we leap into the world of business not only to make profusion of sales but also to make this world a better place to live.
